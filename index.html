Vision


Ek Pahal aims to universalise the resolution in every individuals, prosperity in every family, fearlessness in society and coexistence in nature through the method of education, community involvement and community building.



Mission


To transform our factory based school education system to humane based school education. To create such spaces & platform where people can explore, engage and achieve whatever they want in their life.



Motto


For a healthy environment & better india
